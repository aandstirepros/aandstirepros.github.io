It started with a flat at midnight.

Years ago, Arkan was a long-haul driver pulled over on a frozen shoulder of Highway 1, calling shop after shop. Nobody answered. The one that did wanted three hours and three hundred dollars before they’d even consider the drive. He sat there in the dark and thought: someone should fix this.

Senan was already wrenching on trucks — steady work, fair prices, no upsell theatre. The brothers put their heads together, bought a service truck, and the deal was simple: the phone gets answered. Always. Day, night, weekend, holiday. If you call, we come.

The phone gets answered. The truck gets rolling. The work gets done right. That’s the whole pitch. — Arkan & Senan · Founders

Why mobile only.

We made a choice early on: we don’t run a retail lobby. We don’t sell passenger tires. We don’t do seasonal swaps on minivans. What we do — and what we’re best at — is roll a fully-stocked service truck to a piece of heavy iron that’s sitting dead on a job site, a lease road, or a field, and put it back to work.

That focus means our techs are specialists. OTR work, multi-piece rims, demountable hardware, super-singles, ag floaters — the stuff that breaks a generalist shop is what we do every day. It also means our trucks are stocked for the call, not for a showroom.

What we believe.

A tire service for heavy equipment is a trust business. When your dozer is sitting on a slope or your combine is dead in the middle of harvest, you’re handing somebody the keys to your livelihood. We don’t take that lightly. The price we quote is the price you pay. The work we recommend is the work that needs doing. The number you call is the number that answers.

We’re still small. We like it that way. When you call A&S, you get a real person — sometimes a brother, sometimes a tech who’s been with us for years, never a phone tree. The truck that rolls out is ours. The hands that fix your rig are ours.

Built for the long road.

From two trucks at the start to a fleet now serving 200+ accounts across Alberta — we’ve grown by doing the work the way we said we would. No flash, no franchise, no investor deck. Just a phone that gets answered and a service truck that shows up.
